- Seminar Description -

This seminar will be a reading seminar, working through the book Morse Theory & Floer Homology . We will meet twice a week to discuss that weeks reading. Time permitting, we will study some additional topics in Floer theory: additional structure on the Floer groups, the PSS isomorphism, Lagrangian Floer theory, advanced regularization techniques, and other Floer homology theories in low-dimensional topology and contact geometry are among the candidates.
