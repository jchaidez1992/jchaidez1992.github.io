- REU Description -

This is the homepage for the 2018 Research Experience for Undergraduates (REU) program in geometry and and topology at UC Berkeley. The topics of this REU are smooth, symplectic and contact topology. This program is funded by the National Science Foundation via the UC Berkeley Geometry Research & Training Group (RTG) grant.
