- Meetup Description -

This is a casual discussion group geared towards topics in discrete and computational geometric topology. We will meet two out of three weeks for an hour and a half per week, to discuss our current readings. The main topics will be:

  • basic triangulated manifold theory, and relating PL and smooth structure.
  • discrete morse theory, morse homology and applications
  • normal surface theory
