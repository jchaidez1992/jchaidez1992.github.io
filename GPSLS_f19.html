- Learning Seminar Description -

This is the home page for a graduate student seminar on wrapped Floer homology.

The focus this semester is on the three papers of Ganatra-Pardon-Shende on the wrapped Fukaya category of Liouville sectors (and on the wrapped Fukaya category in general). We also hope to discuss (at some length) the background material in homological algebra and wrapped Floer homology.
