- DRG Description -

This is the homepage for a research-oriented reading group in contact topology. The focus of the semester will be on non-contractible Legendrian sub-manifolds of contactizations of punctured symplectic surfaces. More precisely, we aim to write a paper providing detailed computational methods for and examples of linearized Legendrian contact homology. A broader goal is to conjecture very general vanishing or non-vanishing properties for these linearized homologies.
